About Mrs. Ruth Onoja

Mrs. Ruth Onoja is a passionate gospel musician whose mission is to inspire lives through the transformative power of music. With a heart dedicated to worship, she believes that music is a divine gift that can uplift spirits and bring people closer to God.

Her journey began at a young age, singing in her local church choir, where she discovered her love for gospel music. Over the years, she has performed at numerous events, sharing her message of hope and faith through her songs.

Mrs. Onoja draws inspiration from scriptures such as:

"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord." - Psalm 150:6
"Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth." - Psalm 96:1

Her music is not just entertainment; it is a form of worship that encourages listeners to reflect on their faith and relationship with God. Through her songs, she aims to create an atmosphere of praise and worship, inviting everyone to join in the celebration of God's love.

Join Mrs. Onoja on this beautiful journey of faith and music, and let the melodies of praise fill your heart and soul.
